The Linville Gorge Wilderness, sometimes called "The Grand Canyon of North Carolina", is 11,651 acres of NC wilderness managed by the Forest Service. The wilderness' rock formations, like Table Rock, attract rock climbers of all skill levels. The 39 miles of trails attract hikers and backpackers from across the nation.
